BigTimeAlien is a development and testing application. In some cases, it may temporarily access your basic profile information when you log in using Meta (Facebook/Instagram).

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like any data associated with your use of BigTimeAlien to be deleted, please send an email with the subject line:

“Data Deletion Request – BigTimeAlien”

to:

test@example.com

Include the Following Information

  • Your full name (as it appears on Facebook/Instagram).
  • Your Facebook or Instagram profile URL or ID.
  • A brief statement requesting deletion of data related to BigTimeAlien.

After receiving your request, we will make reasonable efforts to locate and delete any associated data stored during testing.

Automatic Deletion

Because BigTimeAlien is used for development, any temporary data may also be deleted automatically during environment resets or routine cleanup.
